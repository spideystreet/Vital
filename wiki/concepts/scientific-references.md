# Scientific References

All references from open-access or publicly available sources. Categorized by relevance to V.I.T.A.L.

## Physiology & Biomarkers

1. **Chronic stress → burnout: integrative scoping review** / *Frontiers in Psychology, 2025*
   HPA-axis dysregulation, immune impairment, autonomic imbalance, elevated allostatic load.
   [Full text](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2025.1712340/full)

2. **Passive AI Detection of Stress and Burnout** / *Narrative review, 2025*
   HRV, sleep, EDA as passive AI biomarkers in frontline workers.
   [PDF](https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/b342/3e2433bef2ac798f7231f02ef3cddad0d9c6.pdf)

3. **Biomarkers for stress in emergency nurses** / *PMC 2023*
   Hair cortisol + HRV + salivary alpha-amylase protocol.
   [PMC10465916](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10465916/)

4. **Physiological biomarkers of chronic stress: systematic review** / *ICA Health, 2022*
   Hair cortisol most reliable chronic stress biomarker; elevated awakening cortisol in burnout.
   [Full text](https://icahealth.com/physiological-biomarkers-of-chronic-stress-a-systematic-review/)

5. **Stress and HRV: Meta-Analysis** / *PMC/NIH, 2018*
   HRV validated as psychological stress indicator across populations.
   [PMC5900369](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5900369/)

## Wearables & HealthKit

6. **Apple Watch as mental health tracker** / *PMC, 2024*
   Apple Watch validated for HRV, HR, EE, sleep; HRV reflects mild mental stress (<10% error).
   [PMC10853680](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10853680/)

7. **Resting HRV from consumer wearables & health outcomes** / *PMC, 2025*
   Small-to-moderate associations with depressive symptoms, sleep difficulty, mental exhaustion.
   [PMC12693838](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12693838/)

8. **Wearables for burnout detection in healthcare** / *PMC, 2024*
   10 studies reviewed; longitudinal within-person tracking outperforms cross-sectional thresholds.
   [PMC11234055](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11234055/)

9. **Bayesian mixed-effects regression for burnout prediction** / *Semantic Scholar, 2025*
   Nocturnal HRV + sleep stages = strongest burnout predictors; stress reduces deep sleep following night.
   [PDF](https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/d6a0/f0abf25305f09bda3c2aaa26336699d7c553.pdf)

10. **BROWNIE Study** / *PMC, 2024* — Mayo Clinic protocol
    Multi-month wearable (Garmin) + AI study with nurses for burnout prediction.
    [PMC10863108](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10863108/)

## Clinical Proof of Concept

11. **Smartwatch Use and Physician Well-Being: RCT** / *JAMA Network Open, 2025* — Mayo Clinic/Univ. Colorado
    184 physicians, RCT: **54% lower burnout risk at 6 months** with smartwatch use alone (no behavioral prompts). Higher resilience scores.
    [Full text](https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamanetworkopen/fullarticle/2837666)

12. **Real-Time Stress Monitoring: Apple Watch + ML** / *arXiv, 2025*
    12-week RCT: mHELP app (AW HR + ML) reduced acute stress moments; clinically meaningful GAD-7 decline.
    [PDF](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2505.15974.pdf)

## Digital Interventions

13. **Digital burnout interventions: scoping review** / *Frontiers Public Health, 2023*
    7 digital interventions reviewed; combined individual+organizational most effective.
    [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38026413/)

14. **Nurse burnout intervention: systematic review** / *PLOS ONE, 2025*
    MBI-based + digital+physical combined most effective; digital-only has higher attrition.
    [Full text](https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0322282)

## French Market Data

15. **Sleep, physical activity & mental health — French business leaders** / *PLOS Global Public Health, 2026*
    >50% at burnout risk; 10% very high risk; 27% considered suicide.
    [Full text](https://journals.plos.org/globalpublichealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pgph.0005880)

16. **Burnout in ENT France: STROBE analysis** / *ScienceDirect, 2024*
    40% severe burnout in French ENT surgeons; poor sleep + work-life boundary violation = primary risks.
    [Article](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1879729624000577)

17. **Burn-out chiffres clés France** / *Medadom / DARES / Empreinte Humaine, 2024*
    2.5M actifs en burn-out; 480K nouveaux cas/an; coût 2-3 Md€/an.
    [Article](https://info.medadom.com/professionnels-de-la-sante/les-chiffres-cles-du-burn-out-en-france)

## Key Takeaways

- **Clinical proof exists**: JAMA RCT (2025) proves awareness alone prevents burnout
- **Longitudinal > cross-sectional**: within-person tracking essential, not population thresholds
- **HRV + sleep are the strongest predictors** from wearable data
- **Combined digital+human outperforms digital-only** → validates V.I.T.A.L's voice check-in approach
- **French market data is abundant and urgent**: 2.5M affected, €2-3B cost, 53% report high stress
