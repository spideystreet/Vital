## Physiology & Biomarkers

1. **Chronic stress in relation to clinical burnout: an integrative scoping review**
   *Frontiers in Psychology, 2025*
   Confirms HPA-axis dysregulation, immune impairment, autonomic imbalance, and elevated allostatic load as core chronic stress mechanisms.
   [https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2025.1712340/full](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2025.1712340/full)

2. **Passive AI Detection of Stress and Burnout (narrative review)**
   *Semantic Scholar / Nursing Reports, 2025*
   Comprehensive review of HRV, sleep, EDA as passive AI biomarkers in frontline workers.
   [https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/b342/3e2433bef2ac798f7231f02ef3cddad0d9c6.pdf](https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/b342/3e2433bef2ac798f7231f02ef3cddad0d9c6.pdf)

3. **Feasibility of biomarkers to measure stress, burnout and fatigue in emergency nurses**
   *PMC / PubMed, 2023*
   Hair cortisol + HRV + salivary alpha-amylase protocol for objective burnout measurement.
   [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10465916/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10465916/)

4. **Physiological biomarkers of chronic stress: A systematic review**
   *ICA Health, 2022*
   9-study meta-analysis confirming hair cortisol as most reliable chronic stress biomarker; elevated awakening cortisol in burnout patients.
   [https://icahealth.com/physiological-biomarkers-of-chronic-stress-a-systematic-review/](https://icahealth.com/physiological-biomarkers-of-chronic-stress-a-systematic-review/)

5. **Stress and Heart Rate Variability: A Meta-Analysis**
   *PMC / NIH, 2018*
   Meta-analysis validating HRV as a psychological stress indicator.
   [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5900369/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC5900369/)

6. **Physiological outcomes from mind-body resiliency programs**
   *PLOS Mental Health, 2025*
   HRV, HR, blood pressure, respiratory rate as ANS stress markers in healthcare workers.
   [https://journals.plos.org/mentalhealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pmen.0000332](https://journals.plos.org/mentalhealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pmen.0000332)

## Wearables & HealthKit

7. **Effectiveness of the Apple Watch as a mental health tracker**
   *PMC, 2024*
   Apple Watch validated for HRV, HR, EE, and sleep monitoring; HRV reflects mild mental stress.
   [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10853680/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10853680/)

8. **Resting HRV Measured by Consumer Wearables and Health Outcomes**
   *PMC, 2025*
   Consumer wearable HRV has small-to-moderate associations with depressive symptoms, sleep difficulty, and mental exhaustion.
   [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12693838/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12693838/)

9. **Wearable Technologies for Detecting Burnout in Healthcare Workers**
   *PMC, 2024*
   Systematic review of 10 wearable studies; longitudinal within-person tracking outperforms cross-sectional thresholds.
   [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11234055/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11234055/)

10. **Toward burnout prevention with Bayesian mixed-effects regression on wearable data**
    *Semantic Scholar, 2025*
    Bayesian model on consumer wearables: nocturnal HRV + sleep stages = strongest burnout-related predictors; stress reduces deep sleep.
    [https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/d6a0/f0abf25305f09bda3c2aaa26336699d7c553.pdf](https://pdfs.semanticscholar.org/d6a0/f0abf25305f09bda3c2aaa26336699d7c553.pdf)

11. **The BROWNIE Study: Burnout PRedictiOn Using Wearables and AI**
    *PMC, 2024* — Mayo Clinic protocol
    Multi-month wearable (Garmin) + AI study with nurses for burnout prediction.
    [https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10863108/](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10863108/)

12. **Wearable devices and EMA in Work-Related Stress assessment**
    *PLOS ONE, 2026*
    Protocol combining smartwatch physiological data + ecological momentary assessment for work stress.
    [https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article/file?type=printable&id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0339288](https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article/file?type=printable&id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0339288)

## Clinical Proof of Concept — Smartwatches & Burnout

13. **Smartwatch Use and Physician Well-Being: A Randomized Clinical Trial**
    *JAMA Network Open, 2025* — Mayo Clinic / Univ. Colorado
    184 physicians RCT: **54% lower likelihood of burnout** at 6 months in smartwatch group; higher resilience scores. No behavioral prompts — awareness alone was protective.
    [https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamanetworkopen/fullarticle/2837666](https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamanetworkopen/fullarticle/2837666)

## Digital Interventions

14. **Interventions to reduce stress and prevent burnout supported by digital applications: a scoping review**
    *Frontiers in Public Health, 2023*
    Review of 7 recent digital burnout interventions; organizational support critical; individual + organizational combined approaches most effective.
    [https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38026413/](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38026413/)

15. **Person-directed burnout intervention for nurses: A systematic review (PLOS ONE, 2025)**
    MBI-based + digital + physical combined interventions most effective; digital-only has higher attrition.
    [https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0322282](https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pone.0322282)

16. **Real-Time Stress Monitoring with Apple Watch + ML (arXiv, 2025)**
    12-week RCT: mHELP app (Apple Watch HR + ML) reduced acute stress moments in treatment group; clinically meaningful GAD-7 decline.
    [https://arxiv.org/pdf/2505.15974.pdf](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2505.15974.pdf)

## French Market Data

17. **Sleep, physical activity and mental health among French business leaders**
    *PLOS Global Public Health, 2026*
    >50% of French business leaders at burnout risk; 10% very high risk; 27% considered suicide.
    [https://journals.plos.org/globalpublichealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pgph.0005880](https://journals.plos.org/globalpublichealth/article?id=10.1371%2Fjournal.pgph.0005880)

18. **Burnout in ENT France: Update and risk factors (STROBE analysis)**
    *ScienceDirect, 2024*
    40% severe burnout prevalence in French ENT surgeons; poor sleep and work-life boundary violation as primary risk factors.
    [https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1879729624000577](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1879729624000577)

19. **Les chiffres clés du burn-out en France**
    *Medadom / DARES / Empreinte Humaine, 2024*
    2.5M actifs en burn-out; 480,000 nouveaux cas/an; coût 2–3 Md€/an.
    [https://info.medadom.com/professionnels-de-la-sante/les-chiffres-cles-du-burn-out-en-france](https://info.medadom.com/professionnels-de-la-sante/les-chiffres-cles-du-burn-out-en-france)
