# Welcome to Vital

Vital is a **proactive health coach** powered by wearable data and AI. It connects to your Apple Watch, analyzes your biometrics, and delivers personalized coaching — so your health data finally does something useful.

## What Vital does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Wearable Integration" icon="watch">
    Reads 20+ health metrics from Apple Watch via HealthKit — heart rate, HRV, sleep, steps, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Coaching" icon="brain">
    Conversational analysis powered by Mistral, with persistent memory of your history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Morning Brief" icon="sun">
    Daily health diagnosis with adaptive protocols tailored to your personal baseline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Alerts" icon="bell">
    Notifications when your biometrics deviate from your norm — grounded in your history, not generic thresholds.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
